# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: May 4, 2026**

PesaLens ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the PesaLens mobile application.

**1. Data Collection and SMS Permissions**
To provide automated expense tracking, PesaLens requires the `READ_SMS` and `RECEIVE_SMS` permissions. 
* **What we read:** The app strictly parses incoming SMS notifications from Safaricom M-Pesa to extract transaction amounts, merchant names, and timestamps.
* **What we do not read:** We do not read, access, or parse personal text messages, OTPs, or any non-M-Pesa communications.

**2. Local Processing (Zero Data Collection)**
PesaLens is a 100% offline, local-first application. **Your data never leaves your device.** We do not transmit, collect, store, or share your financial data, transaction history, or personal information on any external cloud servers, databases, or third-party analytics services. 

**3. Data Retention and Deletion**
All transaction data and user preferences are stored securely in your device's local storage. You maintain complete control over your data. You can permanently delete all data at any time by using the "Delete All Data" function within the app's Settings menu, or by clearing the app's storage via your Android OS settings.
